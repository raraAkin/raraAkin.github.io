RARA Study abroad

Study abroad

Rara consultancy provides the platform for quality global education placement and services to our clients, who desire and dream to study abroad. We take pride in guiding students locally and internationally from the first stage to the final stage, in the process of leading them to their education destination.
Our experienced education counselors are ready to advise students on courses and career opportunities according to their educational background, interests, aptitude, and aspirations. They have broad network with education providers and can suggest a wide range of study options/programs to meet our clients/ students need.
At Rara Consultancy, we only work with education providers that can offer a high standard of academic achievement and student support. We also stay in regular contact with our students and liaise with schools and parents in monitoring students' progress, so that they can have access to new knowledge, demonstrate ability and skills to work and study in the international environment.
As studying abroad holds a whole new world with a blend of what to study and where to study. Our counselors are trained to correlate and give proper and consistent advice keeping in mind the demand of today’s fast evolving economy. One of our priority is to ensure that our suggested career options are up to date and in accordance with the contemporary industry requirements.
We have strong connections within Portugal, the U.S.A, United Kingdom, Ireland, Spain, Latvia, Estonia, Switzerland, Canada, China and Australia among others. Just choose which University, College or Language school you wish to attend and we will help you realize your objective.

Enjoy the following services on your path to studying abroad;

Consultancy

We help you to choose your study and career
Are you thinking about studying in a foreign university? To study in another language and experience diverse cultures? To book Language course, Bachelor, Master or PhD degree? Just pick a country or city and let us know! We will help you all the way from choosing the right course/program to finding suitable accommodations.

Visas

We drive you step by step to obtain it
Have you booked a program with us? In general, if you apply to study in a foreign country you are required to have a Student Visa. Do you need any assistance in obtaining a visa? Let us help you through your application step-by-step.

Welcome Kit

Move into a new city it's never been so easy
We know how difficult it can be to move to a new country, city and environment. To help you with your transition we provide you with a Welcome Kit full of useful information about the city, local customs, maps, useful contact details and more.

Transfer

Let us help you arrive from airport to home
Have you booked an educational program with us? Do you need a transfer from the Airport? International Port or Train Station? Just let us know and we will give you all the information you need to make your travelling arrangements as smooth as possible.

Accommodation

We can help you find suitable accommodations
Have you booked an educational program with us? Do you need to find accommodation for your studies? We can help you to save time and avoid frustration by helping you find suitable accommodation.
